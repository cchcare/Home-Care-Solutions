Internal Home Care Agency Tool (Excluding Scheduling & Payroll)
1. Client Management

Digital client profiles (demographics, intake forms, care plans, medications, allergies, emergency contacts).

HIPAA-compliant storage of assessments and signed documents.

Progress notes & daily care logs.

Incident reporting system.

Secure document upload (insurance cards, ID, physician orders).

2. Caregiver Management

Staff profiles with demographics, credentials, hire date.

Credentialing/training tracking (CPR, background check, TB, CEUs, etc.).

Alerts for expiring certifications.

HR forms storage (applications, policy acknowledgements, evaluations).

3. Compliance & Regulatory

State-specific compliance checklists (example: PA Chapter 51/611, EVV compliance, etc.).

Audit-ready documentation library.

Policy & procedures repository accessible to staff.

FMLA/Leave documentation tracking.

4. Communication Hub

Internal messaging between office staff and caregivers.

Broadcast announcements (policy updates, reminders).

Family/caregiver portal (secure updates about client care).

5. Task & Workflow Management

To-do lists for office staff (intake, reassessment, training renewals).

Caregiver daily checklist (visit logs, shift reports).

Automated reminders for reassessments and physician order renewals.

6. Forms & Documentation

Digital intake packet (fillable forms).

Incident/accident report templates.

Visit verification (EVV logs without payroll integration).

Electronic signature capability.

7. Training & Resources

Learning module uploads (videos, PDFs, quizzes).

Caregiver handbook access.

Orientation checklist completion tracker.

8. Analytics & Reporting

Client census report.

Caregiver credential compliance report.

Incident trends.

Referral source tracking.

Service utilization (hours authorized vs. hours used).

9. Security & Access Control

Role-based access (admin, supervisor, caregiver, family).

Audit logs of document changes.

HIPAA-compliant encryption.
